Research has actually shown that an intelligently designed and optimised workplace can increase office productivity by over 20%.

The primary difference between us and our competitors is that we specialise in helping our Clients dramatically reduce their real estate and fit-out costs, whilst also enhancing the workplace environment to improve brand identity and increase staff productivity, morale and profit.

With a team of 78 staff directly employed in-house, Fitout Hub is able to provide a team that fits, and who will guide your project through every stage of the way. From concept to completion, you can be confident you’re in safe hands.

We have ample resources to integrate all the key design and project management services, from your scheme’s conception to project completion.

We believe there are many benefits to our in-house team approach (as opposed to the sub-contractors that many of our competitors use) as it allows us to fully control every aspect of the project and draw on a pool of experts from within the company to offer our Clients a unified and carefully considered, comprehensive solution.

Our Clients state they most appreciate the following advantages:

  • Cost and programme clarity and certainty
  • Single point accountability
  • Accelerated process, and the opportunity to fast track certain key tasks
  • Greater flexibility
  • Value for money
  • Better overall control; especially extending through the design and construction phases of the project
  • More effective design methodology
  • Seamless teamwork

Essentially, our approach works like this:

In-House People & Skills

All the key design and project management skills are in-house, meaning we have the ability to carry out the creative and layout work at the same time as we consider technical aspects of the project such as specification and budget.

Qualified & Trained Creative Consultants

Fitout Hub Interiors in-house creative teams are identical to pure design consultancies in terms of skill sets, approach, ingenuity and experience.

All our Designers are qualified Architects or Design Consultants.

We have an in-house state of the art visualisation studio available to all our Designers from which specialised graphics can help you visualise and develop your finished project.

Fast Track Project Development

The whole project can be divided into manageable packages that can be designed concurrently with both technical and budget development to meet tight programme deadlines.

This contrasts with the traditional procurement route that demands the entire scheme must be designed before costing can begin.

Team Management

Each Project Team is managed by its own Senior Partner. The Design, Technical and Construction Teams work together from the outset to resolve every aspect of your project, meaning there are no overlaps and every detail is captured.

Design Control

Design concepts, visuals, colour boards and other presentation material is produced very early in the process so that scheme options can be discussed, adjusted and agreed at the outset.

Clients have total influence over the look and feel of the schemes long before the Maris on site team commences.

Total Health & Safety Responsibility

By undertaking the Principal Contractor and CDM (Construction Design Management) Coordinator roles, for all projects, we shoulder full responsibility for Health and Safety.

Our in-house Health and Safety Manager works closely with our Technical, Construction and Site Management Teams ensuring our construction sites comply fully with all relevant HSE (Health and Safety Executive) legislations, and the CDM Regulations 2007.

We stay current on building codes, mobility and accessibility regulations, and encompass all developing ‘Green’ technologies.

Working Together

The Client has the opportunity to help select their Fitout Hub Project Team to ensure the team working on the project best suits their requirements.

The entire Project Team selected will be introduced to the Client at the outset of the project.

Clear Communication

Fitout Hub Team will receive and communicate all project information, coordinating and liaising with the Client’s own team during weekly meetings and daily updates.

Bespoke Solutions

Another key Fitout Hub strength is our ability and willingness to get to grips with each Client’s particular circumstances and requirements, and then tailor the solution around them through our in-house department from Joinery to Information Technology.

Seamless Process

Having the design and project management skills in-house also means we have the ability to carry out the design work, technical developments, and costing processes simultaneously.

As Fitout Hub design and construct the project in its entirety, there is no division of design responsibility. This ‘one stop’ design responsibility allows the Project Team to be clearly focused on design and programme expectations, and ensures that work is always progressing as expected.

Project Perfect

All Fitout Hub jobs are completed on time, to the highest quality, both satisfying and exceeding our Client’s expectations. Visit “Our Work” to see a large portfolio of recently completed projects.
